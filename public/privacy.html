Privacy Policy

What we store

  • If you invite bot@giftlinks.net as a member to your Ghost site, we store login details for that bot member so that it can fetch paywalled content on behalf of gift link recipients.
  • When a member creates a gift link, we store the post URL and the member's name and email (from their Ghost session token)
  • When someone opens a gift link, we log the timestamp, country (from Cloudflare headers), and referring domain
  • For debugging and operational purposes, we temporarily track all access requests (including IP address and browser information). These logs are automatically deleted after 7 days.

What we don't store

We are not retaining IP addresses, tracking cookies, or any other personally identifiable information about members or visitors beyond what's mentioned above. We do not sell or share any of the information you provide to giftlinks.net to any other company or service.

Analytics

Informational pages on this site like the homepage use Umami, a privacy-focused, cookie-free analytics tool. It collects anonymous page view counts only.

Third parties

This service runs on Cloudflare Workers. All data is stored in Cloudflare D1. No data is shared with or sold to anyone.

Contact

Questions? Email privacy@giftlinks.net.
